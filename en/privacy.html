Privacy Policy

Last updated: 17 June 2026

This policy describes what data the Movly app ("the app", "we") collects, how it handles it and what rights you have as a user. We take privacy seriously — we only collect what's necessary for the app to work, and we never sell your data.

What data we process

Movly is a companion for movies and TV shows — it helps you track what you want to watch, manage lists and playlists, play trailers and keep an overview of title availability. To do that we process the following data:

CategoryWhat it includesWhy
Account dataEmail address and password (stored in a secure, hashed form)Creating an account, signing in and syncing data across devices
Your contentLists, playlists, favorite titles and watch progress (what you've finished)So your lists and progress follow you on every device
ProfilesThe names of the profiles you create within your accountSeparating lists and progress for individual family members
Device diagnosticsDevice type, playback capabilities and approximate connection speedOptimizing display and the quality of played trailers
Operational dataError reports and basic technical logsFinding and fixing bugs, app stability

What Movly does not do: we don't sell or rent your personal data to third parties, we don't use it for targeted advertising and we don't collect precise location, contacts or content from other apps.

Data stored only on your device

Some data stays only on your device and is not sent to our servers:

  • Search history — for faster repeated searches. You can clear it in the app at any time.
  • Settings and preferences — e.g. the selected language and display options.

Third-party services

To work, Movly uses a few external services. We recommend reviewing their policies too:

  • TMDB (The Movie Database) — the source of metadata, posters, images and title information. This product uses the TMDB API but is not endorsed or certified by TMDB.
  • Infrastructure provider (CDN / connection measurement) — for content delivery and an approximate measurement of connection speed.

Movly may contain links to third-party websites. We have no control over their content or privacy practices and take no responsibility for them.

App permissions

The app may request the following permissions:

  • Internet access — required to load information about movies and TV shows and to play trailers.
  • Network state — to adapt quality to the available connection.

Data retention

We keep data associated with your account for as long as the account is active. After the account is deleted we remove it in line with the section below.

Children's privacy

The service is not intended for anyone under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal data from them. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal data, contact us and we will remove it.

Account and data deletion

You can manage your data right in the app. If you want to delete your account and all related data, email us at cinelog@outlook.com with the subject "Account deletion request" and include your registered email address. We'll process the request and delete your data within 30 days.

For the detailed procedure and an overview of which data we delete and which we keep for a limited time, see the dedicated Account deletion page.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We'll notify you of changes by posting the new version on this page with an updated date.

Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, reach out to us:
