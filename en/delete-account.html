Account deletion

Last updated: 17 June 2026

This page explains how to request deletion of your Movly account and all related data, which data will be deleted and which is kept for a limited time.

How to request account deletion

You request deletion of your Movly account by email. Here's how:

  1. Email us at cinelog@outlook.com from the email address registered with Movly.
  2. Use the subject "Account deletion request".
  3. In the body, include your registered email address (and profile name if relevant), so we can identify you unambiguously.
  4. Wait for confirmation — we'll process the request and delete your data within 30 days of verification. We'll let you know by email once it's done.
✉️ Request account deletion
or email us manually at cinelog@outlook.com

What will be deleted

Once the request is processed, we permanently and irreversibly remove the following from our active systems:

  • your account and sign-in credentials (email address, password),
  • all your profiles and their names,
  • your lists, playlists and favorite titles,
  • your watch progress (what you've finished) and preferences,
  • data directly associated with your account.

What we keep and for how long

For security, recovery from backups and to meet legal obligations, we may keep the following for a limited time:

Data typeRetention period
System backupsUp to 30 days after deletion, after which backups containing your data are irreversibly overwritten.
Operational and security logsUp to 90 days; they don't identify you and are deleted once the period ends.
Data required by lawOnly where required by a legal regulation, and only for the strictly necessary period.

Data that stays only on your device (e.g. search history and settings) is removed by uninstalling the app.

Contact

If you have any questions about account deletion or privacy, email us at cinelog@outlook.com. You can find more in our privacy policy.
