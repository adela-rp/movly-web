FAQ

What you're probably wondering

What exactly is Movly?+

Movly is a companion for movies and TV shows. It helps you keep track of what you've watched and what you want to watch, manage lists and playlists, play official trailers and get your bearings on a title's availability.

How much does Movly cost?+

The app is still in development. We'll share details about availability closer to launch.

Which devices does Movly run on?+

On Apple devices (iPhone, iPad, Mac, Apple TV), on Android (phone, tablet, Android TV) and on Windows. The same account and the same data across every platform.

Do my lists sync between devices?+

Yes. Just sign in with the same account and your lists, playlists and watch progress follow you on every device.

What about my privacy?+

We only collect the data needed for the app to work, we never sell it, and you can request account deletion anytime. Details are in our privacy policy.

When is Movly coming out?+

The app is in active development and launch is getting close. Follow this page — we'll let you know.
